Mandazi

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground cardamom (optional)
  • 1/4 cup of melted butter or margarine
  • 1/2 cup of coconut milk or regular milk
  • 1 egg
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix the flour, sugar, baking powder, and cardamom.
  2. Add the melted butter, coconut milk, and egg to the dry ingredients and mix to form a dough.
  3. Knead the dough until smooth and let it rest for 20-30 minutes.
  4. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to about 1/4 inch thickness.
  5. Cut the dough into desired shapes (triangles, circles, or squares).
  6. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
  7. Fry the mandazi pieces in batches until golden brown on both sides.
  8. Remove from oil and place on paper towels to drain excess oil.
  9. Serve warm and enjoy!
