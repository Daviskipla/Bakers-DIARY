Samosa Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1 cup ground meat or vegetables (for filling)
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • Oil for frying

Steps to Make Samosas

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, salt, and vegetable oil. Gradually add water to form a dough.
  2. Cover the dough and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  3. Prepare the filling by cooking ground meat or vegetables with garam masala, turmeric, chili powder, and onions.
  4. Divide the dough into small balls and roll them into thin circles.
  5. Cut each circle in half and form a cone shape.
  6. Fill the cone with the prepared filling and seal the edges with water.
  7. Heat oil in a deep pan and fry the samosas until golden brown.
  8. Remove and place on paper towels to drain excess oil.
  9. Serve hot with your favorite chutney or sauce.
